## Body
> Original WeChat article: https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/p90KBpu53ATVKrDQgyUZcQ
Tether CEO Paolo Ardoino recently sat for a Bankless video interview packed with information. This article summarizes Tether's success formula, future strategy, and impact on the U.S. and crypto industry—with lessons for HKD and RMB stablecoins.
In the digital finance wave, Tether's USDT leads stablecoins. Ardoino shared 2024's $13.7B profit and expects 2025 to exceed it—showcasing commercial strength and Tether's role in global finance, especially as the U.S. GENIUS Act nears passage.
**01 — Staggering Financials: From $13.7B to Potential Trillion-Dollar Valuation**
$13.7B profit (reportedly under 100 staff) from $155B assets at ~5% rates plus gold and Bitcoin. Paolo expects 2025 to beat 2024 with Fed rates high and gold/Bitcoin gains. At Circle's P/E, Tether could theoretically hit $13.7T valuation—above Microsoft and Apple—speculative but stunning.
Reserves: $125B Treasuries (18th-largest holder), 105% liquid reserves vs. banks' ~10%, $176B group equity. Answers transparency doubts and positions Tether for U.S. regulation.
**02 — Stablecoin King: 450M-User Global Network**
USDT market cap ~$150B, 450M users, 30M new wallets per quarter. Success from grassroots emerging-market strategy. During 2020 COVID, inflation and currency collapse drove demand for stable assets. Paolo's story: Argentine youth holding dollars via crypto apps, convincing parents to use USDT instead of black-market dollars.
Distribution from African solar kiosks to South American grocery stores—3B unbanked. Africa: 500 solar kiosks offering USDT, plan 100,000 by 2030 reaching 120M people. "Boots on the ground" spreads dollars in emerging markets; 45M users, 37% use USDT as savings.
**03 — Compliance and New Opportunity: GENIUS Act**
U.S. GENIUS Act is an industry milestone—~80% pass probability on Polymarket. Sets high AML, compliance, and reserve standards aligning with Tether practice. Paolo plans USDT compliance as foreign issuer and may launch U.S.-domestic stablecoin.
GENIUS may become global template. Europe's MiCA requires 60% uninsured bank deposits—Silicon Valley Bank 2023 showed risk. GENIUS's 100%/90% Treasury reserves may push Europe and others toward U.S.-comparable rules.
**04 — Tether's Strategic Value to America: Dollar Hegemony Guardian**
Tether is ally to the U.S. economy. USDT brings financial inclusion to ~500M people where banks don't reach—countering BRICS de-dollarization, especially Africa and Latin America.
As 18th-largest Treasury holder, $125B purchases diversify U.S. debt ownership. vs. China as single holder using debt geopolitically, 450M users spread risk. Reserves at Cantor Fitzgerald; $5B U.S. investments in tech, agriculture, etc.
**05 — Diversified Investments: Bitcoin to AI**
Beyond stablecoins: 100,000 BTC, 80 tons gold, frontier sectors. Plan to become largest Bitcoin miner—protect reserves and network security. Investments in AI (QVAC peer-to-peer AI), biotech (BCI), agriculture (Adeco Agro—major South American landowner), even Juventus FC for distribution.
Tokenized assets: XAUT gold product ~50% of $2B on-chain gold market, backed by 80 tons Swiss vault gold, redeemable for full bars. No oil/silver tokenization planned soon—logistics.
**06 — Tech Innovation: Wallets and Blockchain Ecosystem**
Late 2025: open-source WDK wallet supporting all chains with cross-chain swaps optimizing fees—incentivizing low-fee chains, improving UX. Invests in Bitcoin sidechains (e.g., Plasma) but avoids a single "Tether chain" to encourage competition.
Paolo favors Bitcoin as "first love"—10-minute blocks, low bandwidth suit worst emerging-market conditions. Bitcoin evolves as security anchor; payments on sidechains/L2. Reserves hold Bitcoin only—neutral on other chains.
**07 — 2025: Tether's Redemption Year**
Past transparency and compliance doubts. Growth and compliance efforts shift narrative. DOJ cooperation, frozen illicit funds, 250+ law enforcement partnerships in 55 countries. GENIUS potential pass cements legitimacy; U.S. lawmakers recognize Treasury market and dollar hegemony contributions.
Paolo: "Tether brings financial inclusion to 500M via dollars, supports dollar hegemony, diversifies U.S. debt, invests in U.S. innovation. We create massive value for America and crypto." 2025: continue stablecoin dominance and expand global finance/tech role.
**08 — Lessons: Where Do HKD and RMB Stablecoins Go?**
Paolo Ardoino from a small Italian town grew Tether from 2014 startup to global finance giant. With GENIUS opportunity, capital, distribution, and forward investments redefine stablecoin future. "We're just getting started."
Hong Kong is advancing stablecoin legislation and ecosystem—HKD and offshore RMB stablecoins strategically significant. Regulators signal warmth but roadmap still unclear. The author is researching related directions and will share preliminary results.
