**Digital Gold Rush**
In a recent interview, Saylor discussed the "digital gold rush":
"From 2025 to 2035, this will be the decade of the digital gold rush. Countless new business models, products, services, and thousands of companies will emerge. Like the late-19th-century oil industry—think the 1870s, when people first explored crude oil applications, from kerosene to diesel, asphalt, and other innovations—Bitcoin and digital assets will see similar explosive growth.
This era will be full of experimentation: companies will keep trying different strategies; investors will learn how to position themselves. Innovation will surge, but so will mistakes—people will err, markets will swing, and it is precisely in that chaos that opportunity hides. Those who seize it will create enormous wealth: fortunes will be forged, and many will become billionaires.
This is not a stable period, but a high-risk, high-reward gold-rush age. Like the California Gold Rush or the oil boom of history, participants need courage and vision—but the winners take all. Bitcoin is not the destination; it is the starting point. It will reshape finance, energy, and the global economy.
If you do not act now, by 2035 it may be too late."
