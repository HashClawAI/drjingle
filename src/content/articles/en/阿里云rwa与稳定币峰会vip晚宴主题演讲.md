My name is Michael. I'm a partner at Sunthy Cloud Web3. I'm also a Bitcoin researcher writing on X as Dr. Golden Dog, and founder of Hong Kong Yuance.
**Part I: The Future Is Here—America's "Open Playbook"**
Stretch the time horizon and we may be standing at a historical inflection point. Bitcoin—once dismissed as a fringe asset destined to be "a pipe dream"—is being incorporated into U.S. national strategy as "digital gold." Hong Kong will host a major Bitcoin Asia conference at month-end, drawing participants from around the world. We also see U.S. public companies, ETFs, and institutional investors buying Bitcoin and other crypto assets at scale. This is not just market behavior—it is a signal that America is reshaping global finance through Bitcoin and blockchain.
Meanwhile, the stablecoin market is surging. In 2025, global stablecoin market cap reached $2.7 trillion; optimists project it could soar to $2–10 trillion in coming years.
Dollar stablecoins are backed mainly by U.S. Treasuries. Issuers have become major Treasury buyers—ranked 16th globally. Behind Bitcoin and stablecoins lie U.S. efforts via "Project Crypto" and the GENIUS Act to deeply integrate crypto with traditional finance. Bitcoin in one hand, stablecoins in the other—what is America's open playbook? Obviously: ease the Treasury crisis and reinforce the dollar's world-money status. The presidential family makes some money along the way too.
So someone asks: what are all these stablecoins minted on-chain actually for? Will they collapse?
RWA tokenization answers that question. Tokenizing real-world assets, in my view, is the core supply side of this crypto wave. RWA can put bonds, real estate, art, prediction markets, attention, compute, and more on-chain—"everything on-chain," a global, 24/7 parallel universe platform. Pretty genius, no?
**Part II: Everything On-Chain—The Era of RWA Tokenization**
Caixin put it vividly: "I'm going to wrap my jade rough stone, go to Hong Kong, launch an RWA, and raise thirty to fifty million." A young man from Northeast China was shouting into his phone at Beijing Capital Airport.
The RWA market is exploding. In 2025 it reached $23 billion; by 2030 projections range from $4–16 trillion—does that match stablecoin forecasts?
I classify RWA along two axes: standardized vs. non-standardized, and financial vs. non-financial:
- Standardized financial products (U.S. bonds, government bonds, high-grade corporate debt): ~50% market share
- Non-standardized financial products (receivables, private equity, green bonds): ~20%. Some platforms tokenize PE so users can invest $10 in Musk's SpaceX and xAI—becoming a "Musk investor" for $10, unimaginable in traditional markets
- Non-standardized non-financial assets (real estate, art, carbon credits, IP): ~20%
- Standardized non-financial assets (commodities, precious metals): ~10%
Coinbase VP Max said: "Coinbase is building a one-stop trading platform putting all assets on-chain—stocks, prediction markets, derivatives, RWA—building a faster, global economic foundation." Coinbase's "Everything Exchange" strategy epitomizes this trend.
RWA's appeal: greater liquidity plus blockchain transparency and efficiency, redefining global asset trading rules. We stand at the starting line of the everything-on-chain era.
**Part III: Where Cloud Goes—Alibaba Cloud Ecosystem Upgrade**
So the question returns: in this RWA and stablecoin wave, what role can Alibaba Cloud play? Where does cloud go? What's our path?
My answer: **we go nowhere—we wait right here.** Whether Web3.0, Web4.0, or Web5.0, it all still runs on the World Wide Web; cloud is indispensable.
Alibaba Cloud can actively support ecosystem partners upgrading to provide Web3.0 services for RWA and stablecoin projects.
Alibaba Cloud can offer modular "gold shovel" tools: cloud nodes, cloud tools, cloud compute, cloud computing—and tailor compliance frameworks to different legal jurisdictions worldwide.
Alibaba Cloud can provide chain security and analytics tools to safeguard blockchain ecosystem stability.
Nearly every RWA giant is building its own public chain; every chain needs cloud node support. Cloud is not just blockchain infrastructure—it is an indispensable cornerstone of the blockchain wave.
I often think about ABCD (AI, Blockchain, Cloud, Data) in the digital wave. Cloud sits in the C position—core infrastructure offering unlimited possibilities for RWA and stablecoin ecosystems. Cloud rises, ten thousand chains grow; cloud rises, all things grow.
**Part IV: Light Dance—First Contact in the Multi-Chain Era**
As *First Intimate Contact* puts it: "I dance lightly among the crowded throng. Your strange glances—surprise or admiration—never disrupted my steps." Whether Bitcoin, stablecoins, or RWA tokenization—they are like a butterfly flapping in the Amazon jungle, already stirring a global storm.
Bitcoin, finance, and security are blockchain's eternal three themes. RWA and stablecoins as financial applications are just beginning.
In this era of rising chains, we must embrace change. Shifts in productivity, relations of production, ownership, and contracts may all become "stairs of chaos" pushing history forward.
Distinguished CXOs—everything on-chain, cloud dancing in the wind, the future is here. May you all seize the tech transformation wave within Alibaba Cloud's ecosystem.
Thank you for listening!
